How do I quit Vim?

How do I quit Vim?

To exit Vim, follow these steps:

1\. Press \`Esc\` to ensure you are in normal mode.
2\. Type \`:q\` to quit if you haven't made changes, or \`:q!\` to quit without saving changes.
3\. If you have made changes and want to save them, type \`:wq\` or \`:x\` and press \`Enter\`

This will exit Vim.
